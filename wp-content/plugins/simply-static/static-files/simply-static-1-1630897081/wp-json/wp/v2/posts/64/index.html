Elizabeth Hurley<\/a> knows how to make an entrance.<\/p>\n

On Saturday, the model and actress attended her pal David Walliams\u2019 50th birthday party at Claridge\u2019s Hotel in London.<\/p>\n

The swimsuit designer<\/a> made sure to have all eyes on her as she arrived at the luxury Mayfair venue rocking a shimmering oil spill-hued cocktail dress with a dangerously low v-neck cut that reached down her midriff. The British star completed the look with a matching wide belt to cinch her waist, a glimmering clutch and strappy open-toe stilettos.<\/p>\n

The 56-year-old was all smiles as she rocked tousled tresses to complement her glowy features and signature smoky eyes.<\/p>\n

The night out was a special occasion for Hurley, who usually keeps busy modeling her swimsuit line for her 2.1 million followers on Instagram.<\/p>\n

Most recently, she posed in a seafoam-green one-piece<\/a> as she encouraged her followers to use a discount code to purchase similar “glorious one-pieces” from her collection.<\/p>\n

Hurley launched a London-based swimwear line named Elizabeth Hurley Beach in 2005.<\/p>\n

“I decided to venture into beachwear not only because I\u2019ve always been obsessed with vacation clothes, but also because it\u2019s an area where women, regardless of shape or size, can either look amazing or really get it wrong. I wanted to develop resort collections, which make women feel fabulous at any age,” she announced on the company\u2019s site<\/a>.<\/p>\n

“Looking after my son became my number one task, and it was a joy to have someone else on whom to focus,” Hurley told the magazine. “I was 36 and had been worrying about myself for quite long enough. When it came to launching her swimwear collection, Hurley added, “It allowed me to be more flexible with my time and work when Damian was at school.”<\/p>\n
