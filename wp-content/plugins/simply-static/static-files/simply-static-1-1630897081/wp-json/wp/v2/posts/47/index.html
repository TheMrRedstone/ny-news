Girls Aloud singer Sarah Harding passed away<\/a> early Sunday morning after losing her battle with breast cancer. She was just 39 years old.<\/p>\n

Harding\u2019s mom, Marie, shared the sad news<\/a> of her death on Instagram.<\/p>\n

“It\u2019s with deep heartbreak that today I\u2019m sharing the news that my beautiful daughter Sarah has sadly passed away,” Marie wrote to over 360,000 of Harding\u2019s fans on Instagram. “Many of you will know of Sarah\u2019s battle with cancer and that she fought so strongly from her diagnosis until her last day. She slipped away peacefully this morning.”<\/p>\n

She went on to “thank everyone” for their support since the British singer was diagnosed with late-stage breast cancer in Aug. 2020.<\/p>\n

“It meant the world to Sarah and it gave her great strength and comfort to know she was loved,” Marie continued. “I know she won\u2019t want to be remembered for her fight against this terrible disease \u2014 she was a bright shining star and I hope that\u2019s how she can be remembered instead.”<\/p>\n

Harding released a memoir titled “Hear Me Out”<\/a> four months before passing. In the book the singer revealed that there was one symptom of her breast cancer that she completely dismissed \u2014 pain.<\/p>\n

“At first I thought it was just a cyst. I\u2019d been playing my guitar a lot, and I thought the strap had probably irritated an area around my breast,” she wrote, adding that the pain got increasingly worse over time.<\/p>\n

“It got so bad that I couldn\u2019t sleep in a bed anymore. I slept on the sofa, popping painkillers like they were Smarties,” Harding revealed. “I really overdid it, but the pain was overwhelming. Eventually, my skin started to bruise, and by now I was terrified.”<\/p>\n
